# The digital Mind: How technology is Re-writing the Way We Think

"We outsourced our memory, then forget how to use it". This is not just a dramatic expression, it is the subtle shift occurring across societies globally. As technology is blooming itself to our daily lives, it is transforming not only our way of living but also influences our way of thinking. The externalization of memory and mental efforts to digital digital devices has led to growing depedency. Nowadays we rely on Google for facts, maps for directions and apps for daily or special remainders- and thus, slowly our brain becomes a passive recipient than an active active thinker.
